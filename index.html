About Me

My name is Paulina Rosales. I am an undergraduate student studying at San Jose State University,

pursuingn a Bachelor's Degree in Studio Art, in Preparation for Teaching.

Currently, I am trying to find my style of art.

The artwork I've created has changed and evolved over time.

The artwork I've presented shows the variety of art I have attempted to make.

I usually find something I like and try to create something based off what I am ispired from.

The art pieces I have included on this site are pieces that I have felt proud of, and that define me as an artist and person.
