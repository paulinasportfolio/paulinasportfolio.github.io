Hector, 2019.

This quilt is a very special quilt.

I named it after my boyfriend, Hector, who is battling cancer.

I started this quilt last year in May, and finished it exactly one day before he was diagnosed with rhabdomyosarcoma.

Every hospital visit, and every hospital stay he always takes his quilt to bring him comfort.
